Dengue Case Information System

Web Developer Project

Case Overview

Dengue Fever (DBD) is one of the endemic diseases in Indonesia that requires accurate and timely case recording and reporting. This system was developed to assist health officers or relevant institutions in recording, monitoring, and managing dengue case data digitally, in order to improve the efficiency of monitoring and decision-making processes.

Scope & Goals

  • The system is used by city/district health officers to record dengue patient data.
  • Providing case reports based on region, severity level, and date.
  • Displaying statistical trends of dengue cases through visuals and charts.
  • Improving the accuracy and efficiency of dengue case recording.
  • Providing a strong data foundation for preventive policy-making and treatment strategies.

Summary

This system allows users to import dengue case data by district or sub-district, manage the reports, and display them as charts/statistics, as well as generate reports in PDF format.
